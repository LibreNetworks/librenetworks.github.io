Libre Networks

Open Connections. Free Networks.

Our Mission

Libre Networks is a community-driven initiative dedicated to building and promoting open, equitable, and decentralized communication networks. We believe everyone deserves free access to reliable connectivity — and the knowledge to shape it.

What We Do

  • Open Infrastructure: Developing and sharing open-source networking tools.
  • Community Connectivity: Supporting local mesh networks and digital inclusion projects.
  • Education: Teaching digital freedom, privacy, and open technology skills.

Join the Network

Libre Networks is open to collaborators, developers, educators, and community builders worldwide. Get involved, contribute code, share ideas, or help expand open networks near you.
